Brief Review:

"Paradigm-breaking"

"At times like these a fresh, in-depth, objective and innovative analysis of the entire worldwide panorama is often the best tool at a decision-maker’s disposal. This is exactly what Shodai Sennin J.A. Overton-Guerra offers with “BROKEN ARROW: THE END OF WESTERN LIBERAL DEMOCRACY. Why America Is Imploding – And Why Europe Will Follow”: a uniquely creative, audacious, and integrated study of culture, religion, politics and economics on a global scale, but with special emphasis on the United States, Europe, the Hispanic World and Islam.
Packed with solid statistics, historical references, and irrefutable rational arguments, “BROKEN ARROW” presents a revolutionary theory that integrates religion, culture, society, politics and economics – a Biopsychocultural Theory – which sets a new standard as to how cultures are to be valued and perceived in today’s globally competitive world. For these, and many other reasons, we at MAMBA RYU Publications are very proud to present one of our latest additions to our publication’s catalogue."





Excerpt:

"Like the imagination from where is emerges, the many misperceptions about culture come from a general lack of understanding of its true nature and what it really represents to a human being. Most people think of culture in terms of habits and customs, outfits and attires, songs and dances, foods and drinks, and of course languages and beliefs that characterize an identifiable group of people. These are all no doubt aspects of culture, but they are neither the most definitive nor even the most relevant aspects."

  - Shodai Sennin J. A. Overton-Guerra
