SYMBOLISM

LYNX: is a stealthy animal that sees without being seen and that as a symbol represents perspicacity, sagacity, and vision beyond the evident. Frequently referred to as "the guardian of secrets," its discerning and penetrating gaze contribute towards its 'magical' appearance that reminds us all that "still waters run deep."

GO: The strategy game par excellence of oriental origins in which the objective is to surround the opponent and control space on the board.

THE OWL: A symbol of wisdom, knowledge, health and healing.

CONOCIMIENTO: ("KNOWLEDGE") The power to discern between "what is" and "what seems to be" or "how we would like it to be."

SABIDURIA: ("WISDOM") Knowledge and behavior that lead to happiness and 'eudaimonia.'

THE FOOTPRINTS OF THE LYNX tracing the path of existential strategy as an interminable cycle that leads from "knowledge to wisdom."

STRATEGY: The art of utilizing limited resources to achieve discrete and identifiable ends.

EXISTENTIAL: The life lived in plain awareness of one's own mortality.

THE SNAKE SKIN: A symbol of MAMBA-RYU that refers to the power of renewal, restoration, and vitality.

 

What is Existential Strategy?

PHILOSOPHERS OF ANCIENT GREECE believed that the purpose of life was to obtain 'eudaimonia.' Eudaimonia consists of a state of inner peace, harmony, happiness, personal excellence, of feeling sufficient and enough with who we are and where we are. The combination of knowledge and behavior that lead to this state is known as wisdom.

AS A METHODOLOGY OF LIFE, EXISTENTIAL STRATEGY BEGINS with an identification and understanding of the human existential condition, a comprehension of what are the spiritual needs of the human being and why they arise. From there the student/apprentice begins to learn to identify which of his or her own existential schemas, attachments and desires, beliefs and attitudes, and patterns and behaviors are incompatible with obtaining the goal of eudaimonia - and why.

THE HUMAN EXISTENTIAL CONDITION IS CHARACTERIZED by a state of alienation, of estrangement and discord as much from oneself as from one's surroundings. For this very reason religious and spiritual movements arise that attempt to reunite, reintegrate, and harmonize the individual with self, circumstances, and the universe at large.

UTILIZING THE TECHNIQUES AND METHODOLOGIES OF MAMBA MINDFULNESS AND IMAGINOCEPTIVE META-PROGRAMMING, exploring the existential issues of 'being' and 'becoming' of the human condition, and guided by the motto "From Knowledge to Wisdom" Master/Founder Overton has created a comprehensive and unparalleled program dedicated to direct a student/apprentice along the Way to eudaimonia.
