THE KAI IS A NEW WEAPON CREATED BY MASTER/FOUNDER OVERTON as a complement to the empty-hand technique of Black Mamba.

KAI-JUTSU IS A WEAPON SYSTEM useful not only as means for personal protection, but also as tool to learn many other common and improvised weapons.

'KAI' is a word that means "innovation," "to surround," and "ocean," all of which are concepts that are well-represented in KAI JUTSU as this innovative weapon lends itself to surrounding and overwhelming an opponent in a never-ceasing flow of blows and attacks.
