CANCELLATION & REFUND POLICY

In the event something unexpected occurs which requires you to cancel or reschedule an upcoming class or appointment, Baby Senses will always work in good faith to reschedule your bookings without a charge to you. Classes or appointments can also be directly rescheduled online through the website more than 24 hours before the class or appointment timing; to cancel within 24 hours of your class or appointment, please call Baby Senses directly at +971.4.5587307 or send an email to bookings@babysenses.me. Please note that any no-shows or cancellations within 24 hours of your scheduled class or appointment may be subject to a late cancellation charge of up 100% of the cost of the applicable class or appointment.
