The Five Spheres Of Mamba-Ryu

The EARTH SPHERE is dedicated to the body, to the physical existence of the individual. It is the Sphere of BLACK MAMBA, the combative and martial branch of MAMBA-Ryu dedicated to personal protection. Enclosed in BLACK MAMBA is KAI-JUTSU, a physical weapon and combative method recently created by Master-Founder Overton.

The WATER SPHERE emphasizes the importance of the synergistic relationship between 'the individual' and 'his circumstances,' as well as the environmental, social, and ecological context that they encounter. This is the domain of GREEN MAMBA which comprises the program of "urban survival" that in its way overlaps with BLACK MAMBA.

The FIRE SPHERE represents the conscious/cognitive facet of the mind and its development, stressing management of the mental processes to confront the real world with clarity and understanding. This is the domain of MAMBA MINDFULNESS.

The WIND SPHERE comprises the non-conscious and emotive aspects of the mind. Its operation is the domain of IMAGINOCEPTIVE META-PROGRAMMING, also known as HYPNOTIC-SHAMANISM.

Finally, the VOID SPHERE is the Tao of Mamba, the philosophical-spiritual schema that includes and integrates the other Spheres, fusing their understanding into an integral and cohesive whole. This Sphere is expressed in the program of EXISTENTIAL STRATEGY.

 

What is Mamba-Ryu?

INTEGRATING ALL THE PROGRAMS of Black Mamba, Kai-Jutsu, MAMBA Mindfulness, Imaginoceptive Meta-Programming, and Existential Strategy, MAMBA-RYU is the most complete personal development program in existence, combining martial art, philosophy of life, spiritual way and existential survival strategy.

MAMBA-RYU IS THE INTEGRAL PARADIGM OF THE 'MYSTIC-WARRIOR' dedicated to guiding the student/apprentice in challenging the existential issues of being (what am I?, what should I be?, how do I change?) and becoming (where am I?, where should I go?, how do I get there?).

Spirit is mind and body in action.

J. A. GUERRA OVERTON - "THE FOUNDER"
